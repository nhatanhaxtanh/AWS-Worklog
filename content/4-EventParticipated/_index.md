## [Event 7 — AI-Driven Development Life Cycle: Reimagining Software Engineering](4.7-Event7/)  
- **Date:** 18/09/2025  
- **Location:** Ho Chi Minh City / Online  
- **Role:** Attendee  
- **Summary:** Introduction to AI-Driven Development Life Cycle (AI-DLC), a transformative methodology that positions AI as a central collaborator throughout software development. The event covered the three phases (Inception, Construction, Operations), AI-powered execution with human oversight, and introduced Kiro—an AI-native IDE featuring spec-driven development, hooks for automated oversight, and synchronization between specs and code.  
- **Outcome / Lessons Learned:** Understood how AI-DLC reimagines software engineering by placing AI at the center of development, learned about the three-phase structure with AI-human collaboration, recognized potential for development cycles to shift from weeks to hours/days, and gained insights into Kiro's production-ready tooling that bridges prototyping and production systems.
